About Indah

One of the top hospitality chains in the world...



By increasing the number of our brands we are adding more to our portfolio and also increasing our Indah-family members.

Indah was founded in 1990, when Venkatesh Iyer, one of our founders, converted his beach row house into a small accommodation for visitors. Later, when his hospitality became famous, he and his 2 friends, Shreya Maheshwari, and Alok Rathod, realised that this could be the beginning of something big. And that's when Indah was born.




Why Choose Indah?


Indah is not just the place for those looking for an authentic and peaceful getaway, but also for those who are always on the go and need a professional and reliable stay. Our staff and services are primarily dedicated towards making our customers feel at home. Today we have 10000+ happy customers and hotels across 10+ countries. Caring for people and their comfort is our core value. Due to our customer's well wishes today Indah is one of the best hospitality chains across the world.

Saving the dying art


Many artisans and craftsperson are losing their jobs and identity just because of reducing value of their skill in market. We try to extend a helping hand towards these people by selling their products at Indah shop, so that they can get back to work and their skill would be appreciated.

Making big dreams come true


These eyes are filled with dreams and ambition, Indah hotels are donating 20% of their revenue to these little ones; so that they can live their big dreams. Come and be the part of this delightful change.

One step towards sustainability


Indah-hotels believe in healing the world and reducing the burden on mother earth. The biggest burden on our planet is the increase in landfills. We believe in making a change; hence we are trying our best to reduce it. We are trying to be eco-friendly by cutting the amount of waste.

We care for our employees


We hire experienced people and excel in our quality of service. We are always open to employ people having professional approach and increase our Indah-family. Do come and join us!


Indah-Community


Now Indah's family members are across 10+ countries in the world. We care about every community attached to us and try our best to maintain the level of our service without hurting their sentiments. Come and join us today!

Happy moments with us


Be yourself at Indah-hotels/villas. Book your staycation with us and we promise a great experience to you. Amazing experience awaits..
