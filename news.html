2. Indah goes Eco-friendly



The bathroom supplies of hotels consist of plastic. Plastic is non-biodegradable which apparently increases the landfill, adressing this problem Indah has started promoting eco-friendly habits. They will mainly comprise of:

  • Donating extra food to the organizations which will distribute it among the poor people.


  • Providing non-plastic supplies.


  • Saplings as complimentary gift.


  • Plastic-free cleaning agents.


  • Solar power generation.


  • Battery operated vehicles in the premises.


  • E-bill system to reduces usage of paper.


There are many such initiatives taken by Indah-hotels which are not just customer friendly but also nature friendly. Plastic-free cleaning agents are actually glass bottles which are filled with solutions which are provided in the bulk store. Indah doesn't encourage the usage of plastic bottles in their premises hence there would be no water-bottle purchases allowded in the hotels, Instead the staff highly recommonds to bring your own bottle or purchase eco-friendly bottles from the Indah store which would be available in every Indah hotel; refill your bottles from the water coolers available in hotel. The staff charges Rs 20 for any leftover found due to which the customers won't waste their food. Indah has started installing the water-flow reducers in every bathroom which will reduce the amount of water wastage.
