Relax.Rejoice.Rejuvenate.

"Keitha" means forest/woodlands. Keitha is a brand which includes the hotels in greenery and forests. These hotels will surely connect you with nature and disconnect you from toxicity of outer worlds. Keitha hotels are meant to detox you by providing meditation centres, yoga centres and healthy diet. For your on the go work needs, Wi-Fi equipped workplaces are at your service. However, since we are bounded with forest rules and regulations/ property of nature, we don't want to hurt the birds by the toxic waves of wi-fi, hence working stations are few kilometres away from the forest. We hope you respect nature and understand the policy followed by Keitha-hotels. Keitha hotels are ready to welcome you in nature's lap.



Enjoy sitting and lounging in nature. Spend your "Me time" at Keitha lounge which is full of books, Coffee vending machine and much more. Sit back, relax and explore your inner abilities.

Reconnect with nature; book a private Keitha Villa which includes private open to sky Jacuzzi, private BBQ, and much more. We promise you a calm and peaceful staycation.

Rejoice yourself in the lap of mother earth. Forest view pools are just perfect to connect you with nature.

Physical health is as important as mental health. At Keitha's yoga centers, we believe in making your stay healthier.

A healthy trip is incomplete without healthy food. Green nature surely provides you organic food, select your preferences from our healthy diet menu at Keitha. Our menu is designed specially for you by top level nutritionist, so that you won't compromise on your health.

Keitha consists of nature friendly workspaces which are in outer parts of forests so that we can balance work and nature together.

Sunrays are ready to bring smile on your face. These attics are perfect enhance your experience of forest stays.
